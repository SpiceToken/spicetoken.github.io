Proof of Fren (POF)

What is Proof of Fren?

Proof of Fren is a way to see if someone you are tipping is a complete shitbag who doesn't flow the spice, or is indeed a good Fren and gives as good as they get.

Here is how it "currently" works:

  • total tips received = total tips received/2
  • (total tipped to others/total tips received)*100 = n(%)
  • n/20 = X/5 rating
  • 6/5 the highest achievement, and applies to anyone who has tipped more than 50% of total Spice received.

This will be refined over time to include valuable logic.

To invoke Proof of Fren, simply add "pof" to the very end of your tip.

Example: 100 spice pof

Example: tip 100 pof

Ok but what does it all mean?:

  • 0/5 PoF = you suck
  • 1/5 PoF = total tipped vs total received ratio of 10% (Not Horrible)
  • 2/5 PoF = total tipped vs total received ratio of 20% (Better)
  • 3/5 PoF = total tipped vs total received ratio of 30% (Omg)
  • 4/5 PoF = total tipped vs total received ratio of 40% (Whaaat?!?)
  • 5/5 PoF = total tipped vs total received ratio of 50% (NOOICE!!!)
  • 6/5 PoF = total tipped vs total received ratio greater than 50% (GOD MODE)

SpiceFeed Privacy

Telegram

If you are an admin of a private or public telegram group and want to allow tipping in your group without those tips showing up on SpiceFeed, simply invoke the following commands in your group, after adding @SpiceTippingBot.

/spicefeedon - Allow tipped content in SpiceFeed

/spicefeedoff - Keep tipped content off of SpiceFeed
