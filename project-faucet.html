Overview

Spice faucet allows anyone to grab a bit of spice every 24 hours.

Input your BCH SLP address to receive random amounts ranging from 20-100 Spice!

The faucet has features such as potentially receiving 0 Spice (Ouch!), with some lucky people receiving 1000 Spice (Nice!)
